# 50 New Features for Tycoon Game

## Core Gameplay Features (1-10)

1. **Prestige System** - Reset progress to gain permanent multiplier bonuses. Players can reset their game and gain a prestige bonus that multiplies all income by a set percentage based on total cash earned before reset.

2. **Offline Earnings** - Earn passive income while the game is closed. Calculate time away and reward players with accumulated cash when they return.

3. **Achievement System** - Unlock badges and milestones (e.g., "Earn $1M", "Buy 100 Workers", "Get 50x multiplier") with cosmetic rewards.

4. **Daily Rewards** - Log in daily to claim cash bonuses, multiplier boosts, or special items. Reset daily at midnight.

5. **Seasonal Challenges** - Limited-time objectives (e.g., "Earn $5B in 10 minutes") with exclusive rewards and leaderboard rankings.

6. **Factory Chain System** - Build linked production chains where one producer's output feeds into another, creating complex optimization puzzles.

7. **Auto-Clicker Feature** - Automatically click the main button at configurable intervals. Costs cash to unlock and can be upgraded.

8. **Milestone Bonuses** - Earn special cash/multiplier boosts when hitting specific producer quantities (e.g., 10, 50, 100 of each type).

9. **Research Tree** - Tech tree where players unlock new features by spending cash. Each research unlocks new mechanics or improvements.

10. **Event System** - Random events occur (market crash, surge, lucky find) that temporarily modify income, costs, or provide one-time bonuses.

## Enhancement & Customization (11-20)

11. **Statistics Dashboard** - Track total cash earned, clicks made, time played, average income, current session stats, and all-time records.

12. **Theme System** - Switch between light/dark themes and custom color palettes. Save preferences in localStorage.

13. **Game Settings** - Configure auto-save frequency, animations toggle, sound on/off, notification preferences, and UI scale.

14. **Tooltip System** - Hover information showing detailed stats for producers, upgrades, and features. Explains what each item does.

15. **Producer Specializations** - Add sub-categories to producers (e.g., Workers: Miner, Builder, Farmer) with unique bonuses when mixed together.

16. **Visual Effects** - Particle effects for clicks, purchases, level-ups, and achievements. Customizable effect intensity.

17. **Sound Effects Overhaul** - Unique sounds for different producer types, upgrade purchases, achievement unlocks, and events.

18. **UI Animations** - Smooth transitions, number counting animations, pulse effects on buttons, and fade-in/fade-out transitions.

19. **Responsive Design** - Optimize layout for mobile, tablet, and desktop with touch-friendly buttons and adaptable UI.

20. **Accessibility Features** - Dark mode by default, high contrast option, larger text sizes, keyboard shortcuts, and screen reader support.

## Economic Mechanics (21-30)

21. **Dynamic Pricing** - Producer prices increase based on market demand (how many players own). Affects your personal pricing too.

22. **Loan System** - Borrow cash with interest. Loans must be repaid over time but help overcome cash shortages early game.

23. **Dividend System** - Passive cash generation based on total producers owned. Scales independently from upgrades.

24. **Tax System** - Pay a small percentage tax on income. Higher income = higher tax rate. Encourages spending cash or multiple playthroughs.

25. **Economy Cycles** - Market enters boom/bust cycles affecting all producer income by ±20-30% for 60 seconds at a time.

26. **Rare Item Drops** - Randomly drop special items during gameplay that can be sold for bonus cash or traded for permanent bonuses.

27. **NPC Traders** - NPCs offer limited-time deals to buy/sell cash at favorable rates. Refreshes periodically.

28. **Market Speculation** - Predict price movements and place bets for cash rewards. Introduces risk/reward strategy.

29. **Inflation Mechanic** - As you earn more, prices and income scale to maintain progression challenge. Counteracts soft caps.

30. **Debt System** - Owe money to unlock certain producers. Must earn cash to pay off debt before unlocking the next tier.

## Progression & Goals (31-40)

31. **Quest System** - Daily/weekly quests (e.g., "Buy 5 Apprentices", "Earn $50K") with cash and XP rewards.

32. **Level System** - Gain XP from clicks, purchases, and upgrades. Level up to unlock new features and producers.

33. **Prestige Levels** - Unlock new prestige tiers with bigger multipliers after each reset. Encourages multiple resets.

34. **Milestone Tracker** - Visual progress bars for unlocking next producer, next upgrade, or next achievement.

35. **Campaign Mode** - Story-driven progression through "chapters" with unique rules, challenges, and narrative context.

36. **Skill Trees** - Spend earned "skill points" on permanent passive bonuses (e.g., +1% income per level in "Efficiency" tree).

37. **Boss Encounters** - Optional difficult challenges requiring large cash amounts to defeat, granting exclusive rewards.

38. **Secret Achievements** - Hidden challenges (e.g., "Click 10,000 times in one session") with surprise rewards when unlocked.

39. **Endgame Goals** - High-level targets (e.g., "Reach $1 Quadrillion", "Buy all producers 100x") with meta-rewards.

40. **New Game+ Mode** - Start over with bonuses from previous playthrough. Unlock harder difficulties and exclusive content.

## Social & Multiplayer (41-45)

41. **Friend Leaderboards** - Compare stats with other players. View their progress, strategies, and achievements.

42. **Shared World Events** - Server-wide events where all players contribute to a goal. Rewards unlock when goal is met.

43. **Trading System** - Trade cash or items with other players. Escrow system prevents fraud.

44. **Guilds/Clans** - Join groups with shared perks. Guild members get bonuses when playing together.

45. **Chat System** - In-game global and guild chat. Share strategies, celebrate wins, and build community.

## Technical & Polish (46-50)

46. **Cloud Saves** - Save progress to an account and sync across devices. Never lose progress again.

47. **Replay System** - Record and watch replays of impressive runs or speedruns with stats overlay.

48. **Undo System** - Undo your last 5 purchases/upgrades (limited uses per session) to fix accidental mistakes.

49. **Import/Export Saves** - Export save as JSON file and import on other devices. Backup your game manually.

50. **Modding Support** - Simple API to allow custom producers, upgrades, and events. Community-created content system.

## Implementation Priority Suggestion

**Phase 2 (Depth):** Quest System, Level System, Factory Chain, Research Tree, Skill Trees
**Phase 3 (Polish):** Themes, Sound Effects, Animations, Responsive Design, Settings Menu
**Phase 4 (Advanced):** Cloud Saves, Trading, Guilds, Events, Endgame Goals
**Phase 5 (Future):** Modding, Replay System, Campaign Mode, New Game+
